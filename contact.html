Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with your AI tools?

+

Simply visit our homepage and click on any tool that interests you. Most tools are ready to use immediately with no signup required. For advanced features, you may need to create a free account.

Are your AI tools free to use?

+

We offer both free and premium tiers. Basic usage of most tools is free, while advanced features and higher usage limits require a subscription. Check individual tool pages for pricing details.

Is my data secure when using your tools?

+

Yes, data security is our top priority. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't store personal information unless necessary for tool functionality, and we never share your data with third parties.

Can I integrate your tools with my existing workflow?

+

Many of our tools offer API access for integration. We're also working on Zapier and Make.com integrations. Contact us if you need specific integration requirements.

How accurate are your AI responses?

+

Our AI models are trained on high-quality data and continuously improved. While AI responses are generally accurate, we recommend reviewing important information. The AMA Bot for SBI Card queries has been specifically fine-tuned for accuracy.
